About SEEDHE-MAUT

Tera Bhai Undefeated Seedhe Maut is an Indian hip-hop music duo consisting of two talented artists, Encore and Calm. They are known for their unique and innovative approach to rap and hip-hop music in India. Seedhe Maut's music often explores social and political issues, personal experiences, and urban life in India, and they have gained recognition and a dedicated fan base for their thought-provoking lyrics and distinctive style.
